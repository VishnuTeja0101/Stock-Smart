STOCK SMART: Diet-Based Smart Grocery Planning and Auto-Replenishment System
Problem Statement
In today's fast-paced world, many working professionals and families follow specific diet plans to maintain a healthy lifestyle. These diet plans require a consistent supply of ingredients such as vegetables, fruits, grains, dairy products, and protein sources. However, users often do not have sufficient time to monitor grocery inventory at home and manually check whether all required ingredients are available for their upcoming meals.
Existing grocery delivery platforms allow users to purchase groceries online, but they do not analyze a user's diet plan, track household inventory, or identify shortages of ingredients required to follow the planned diet. As a result, users must manually compare their diet plans with available grocery stock, create shopping lists, and place orders whenever items are unavailable. This process is time-consuming and may lead to interruptions in diet schedules, missed meals, or last-minute purchases.
To address this issue, STOCK SMART proposes a web-based intelligent grocery planning system that integrates diet planning with household inventory management. The system allows users to enter their diet plans and maintain a digital inventory of groceries available at home. Based on the selected diet plan, the system automatically identifies the ingredients and quantities required for future meals and compares them with the available stock.
Whenever an ingredient is unavailable or insufficient, the system generates a purchase recommendation and prepares a shopping list automatically. Before placing any order, the system notifies the user and requests confirmation. Upon approval, the user can proceed to purchase the required items through supported online grocery delivery platforms.
The proposed system aims to automate grocery planning, reduce manual effort, prevent ingredient shortages, help users consistently follow their diet plans, and improve overall convenience in managing household food requirements.
Objectives


Allow users to create and manage personalized diet plans.


Maintain a digital inventory of grocery items available at home.


Automatically identify ingredients required for the selected diet plan.


Compare required ingredients with available stock levels.


Detect missing or insufficient ingredients automatically.


Generate smart shopping recommendations and grocery lists.


Notify users before placing any grocery order.


Obtain user confirmation before proceeding with purchases.


Reduce manual grocery management and planning efforts.


Help users maintain uninterrupted adherence to their diet plans.


Expected Outcome
The system will provide an intelligent solution that connects diet planning with grocery inventory management. By automatically detecting ingredient shortages and generating purchase recommendations, STOCK SMART ensures that users always have the necessary ingredients available to follow their diet plans while minimizing manual effort and improving convenience.
